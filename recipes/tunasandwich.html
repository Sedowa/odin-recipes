Tuna Sandwich

Description

A good lunch to take to work.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread of your choice
  • 2 tablespoons of mayonnaise
  • 1 can of tuna
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic or onion powder
  1. Mix tuna, mayonnaise and garlic or onion powder into bowl.
  2. Spread generously onto bread.
  3. Enjoy!
