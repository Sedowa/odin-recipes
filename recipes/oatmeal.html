Oatmeal

Description

Plain oatmeal for when you just need a healthy dose of fiber to start your day.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of oats
  • 1 Tblspoon of Flaxseed
  • 2 Cups of water
  1. Put oats, flasxeed and water into a bowl.
  2. Microwave for 1 to 1-1/2 minutes.
  3. Stir before serving.
