Scramble

Description

A mouth watering mixture of fried potatoes, scrambled eggs, sausage and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 12 Eggs
  • 4 Sausages
  • 3 Potatoes
  • 1 Block of Cheese
  1. Fry the potatoes in a large skillet on medium-high heat until they start browning evenly. Stir as needed.
  2. Scramble the eggs in a large bowl. You can optionally add a dash of salt and pepper here.
  3. Add in sausage to cook alongside the potatoes. Continue stirring.
  4. When meat cooks to your liking pour eggs over the whole skillet and let them cook.
  5. Continue to stir the whole thing to prevent burning of the eggs.
  6. Add cheese on top and turn heat to low or off. Put a lid on skillet to allow cheese to melt.
  7. Finished!
